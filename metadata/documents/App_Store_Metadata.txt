APP DESCRIPTION (APPLE)
----------------------------------
Master Quran memorization (Hifz) with AI-powered mistake correction and real-time feedback.

Tarteel - Hifz AI is your personal Quran companion, designed to bridge the gap between listening and active recitation. Whether you are revising a specific Surah or memorizing for the first time, our advanced AI listens to your recitation and provides real-time feedback, helping you correct mistakes instantly.

Perfect for beginners, students, and families - our clean, distraction-free Mushaf interface makes Quran learning accessible and easy for kids and adults.


AI-POWERED TARTEEL CORRECTION

Act as your own teacher with our state-of-the-art AI assistant.

- Real-Time Feedback: The app listens to your Arabic recitation and highlights mistakes in Red and correct words in Green
- Privacy First: All AI processing happens offline on your device. Your voice data is never sent to the cloud
- Silence Detection: Automatically stops recording when you finish an Ayah
- Repeat Until Correct: Challenge yourself to loop an Ayah until you achieve perfect Tajweed


ADVANCED AUDIO PLAYBACK

Customize your listening experience to suit your learning speed.

- Adaptive Delay: Automatically adjusts the pause between Ayahs based on their length
- Custom Range Practice: Select specific verses (e.g., Ayah 10-15) to create focused loops
- Loop & Repeat: Set custom ranges, repeat single Ayahs, or loop entire Surahs
- Speed Control: Adjust playback speed from 0.5x to 2.0x
- Background Play: Continue your Quran offline listening even when the screen is off


COMPLETE QURAN LIBRARY

- Full Access: All 114 Surahs with high-quality audio from expert Qaris
- Offline Support: Download Surahs on-demand and practice anywhere without internet
- Visual Comfort: Choose from 7 beautiful font scripts (Uthmanic, IndoPak, Naskh) and adjust text size
- Smart Navigation: Search by Surah name or number and jump instantly to any Ayah


PRIVACY & CONVENIENCE

- No Sign-Up Required: Start using the app immediately with anonymous login
- On-Device Processing: Your voice recordings stay on your device - never uploaded
- Storage Management: Easily manage downloads to save space
